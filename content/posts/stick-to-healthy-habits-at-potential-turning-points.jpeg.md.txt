Inflection points break patterns and mark a notable change. In life these can be brought on by changes in location, work, physical condition, personal relationships and more.

Inflection points can be positive or negative. Stick to the good habits at times of change. Don't allow the changes to make it a negative inflection point. Develop a new positive trajectory.
